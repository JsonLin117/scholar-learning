# PMC (Production Management & Control) — SC Daily #8

## One-line Definition
PMC = PC (Production Control: scheduling + progress tracking) + MC (Material Control: MRP + procurement follow-up)

## Core Functions
- PC: production scheduling, capacity planning, delivery management
- MC: MRP calculation, purchase requisition, material dispatch, excess/obsolete control

## Scheduling Principles
1. Delivery date priority
2. Customer classification (CSP first)
3. Capacity balance across stations
4. Process complexity awareness

## DE Opportunities
- MRP anomaly detection
- Real-time WIP dashboard (Kafka + Flink)
- Shortage prediction model
- Forecast vs Actual demand analysis
